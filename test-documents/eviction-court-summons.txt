1. NON-PAYMENT OF RENT: Defendant has failed to pay rent in the amount of
   $2,400.00 for the month of January 2026, and $2,400.00 for the month of
   February 2026, for a total outstanding balance of $4,800.00, despite
   having received a lawful 3-Day Notice to Pay Rent or Quit on January 5,
   2026, pursuant to California Code of Civil Procedure § 1161(2).

   a 30-Day Notice to Quit served on December 1, 2025, pursuant to California
   Civil Code § 1946. Defendant has failed to vacate the premises as required.
